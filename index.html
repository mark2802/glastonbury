Artist Spotlights

As detailed in the report, from rave icons to genre-defining pioneers, these are some of the key players shaping the sound of Glastonbury 2025. Their presence highlights the festival's commitment to showcasing both the history and future of electronic music.

The Prodigy

Synonymous with 90s rave, their aggressive fusion of breakbeat, techno, and punk energy continues to captivate massive audiences, headlining the Other Stage.

Fabio & Grooverider

Undisputed pioneers of D&B and jungle. Their performance with The Outlook Orchestra elevates the genre, celebrating their immense legacy.

Nile Rodgers & Chic

The architects of disco. Their presence on the Pyramid Stage underscores the timeless, unifying power of disco in the evolution of all dance music.

PinkPantheress

A modern innovator blending D&B and 2-step garage with alt-pop. Her set exemplifies the festival's embrace of emerging, genre-redefining artists.
